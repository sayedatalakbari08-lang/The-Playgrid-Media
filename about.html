Our story

Management that thinks beyond the next post

PlayGrid Media started with a frustration that many creators know well. The opportunities were there, but the support was not. Deals were rushed, value was left behind, and no one was thinking about the next year, only the next campaign.

So we built a different kind of management company. One that treats every creator as a long term partner, keeps the roster small on purpose, and measures success by the careers we help build, not the contracts we churn through.

We stay intentionally broad. We represent talent across many spaces and connect them with brands that fit, wherever that creator is headed next.
